What is PlastiStar?

PlastiStar is a digital tool to support plastic waste segregation and awareness. Users capture photos of plastic items, classify them into categories, and earn reward points in a virtual wallet for every responsible action.

Built For

Students, households, and communities who want a simple entry point into plastic waste management and environmental action.

Core Idea

Make sustainability feel rewarding, visual, and easy to understand instead of abstract.

The Global Plastic Problem

Plastic waste is one of the most pressing environmental challenges we face today. A huge fraction of single-use plastics ends up in landfills, oceans, and informal dumpsites, where it can persist for hundreds of years.

  • Microplastics are now found in water, soil, and even human bodies.
  • Improper segregation makes recycling costly and inefficient.
  • Awareness is still low about plastic codes and safe disposal methods.

How PlastiStar responds

  1. Helps users identify different plastic types through simple categories.
  2. Connects each action to a reward system, reinforcing positive behaviour.
  3. Acts as an educational tool for schools, colleges, and community drives.

Reward System – How Your Eco-Actions Become Points

PlastiStar assigns a score to each plastic item you correctly identify and log. Different categories of plastic correspond to different points based on their impact and difficulty in recycling.
